Sustainability in Sports

A Green and Inclusive Approach

The Paris 2024 Olympics are set to be the most sustainable Games in history. With a focus on reducing carbon emissions and minimizing environmental impact, Paris will showcase eco-friendly innovations, including renewable energy-powered venues and zero-waste initiatives. Inclusivity is also at the heart of the Games, as Paris strives to make this event accessible to all, from athletes with disabilities to fans across the globe. By embracing sustainability and diversity, the Paris 2024 Olympics aim to set a new standard for future global sporting events.

Sustainable Celebration

The Paris 2024 Olympics will blend iconic venues with sustainable practices, creating a unique and eco-friendly global celebration of sports and unity.
